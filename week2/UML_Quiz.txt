Describe the purpose of use case diagram?
- A use case diagram shows the dynamic aspects of a system. They are used to model the outside view of the system, and help to identify the internal and external factors influencing the behavior of the system.

How object oriented concepts are related to UML?
- UML diagrams are representations of OO concepts. Objects are real-world entities that exist, and the basic concepts of OOP (encapsulation, etc) can be represented in UML.

Is component diagram representing the dynamic view of a system?
- No. A component diagram is one of the four structural diagrams.

Name major building blocks of UML?
- Things, Relationships, Diagrams
